PECA Law Project

Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA), Pakistan

This project presents a comprehensive study of the Prevention of Electronic Crimes Act (PECA), focusing on its role in regulating cyber activities in Pakistan. It highlights key sections, legal implications, and real-world applications of the law in modern digital environments.

Objectives
  • To understand the fundamentals of cyber laws in Pakistan
  • To analyze the major provisions of PECA
  • To evaluate the impact of PECA on individuals and organizations
  • To explore challenges and criticisms of the law
Key Topics Covered
  • Introduction to Cybercrime
  • Overview of PECA
  • Major Offenses under PECA
  • Cyber Terrorism & Data Protection
  • Electronic Fraud & Identity Theft
  • Legal Procedures & Punishments
  • Case Studies and Real-life Examples
Importance of PECA

PECA plays a crucial role in ensuring cybersecurity, data protection, and digital ethics. It helps prevent online harassment, fraud, and misuse of digital platforms, making the internet a safer place.

Technologies Used

Microsoft PowerPoint / Word (for documentation) | Research Papers & Online Legal Resources

Project Type

Academic Project – Professional Practices (BS Computer Science)

Conclusion

This project emphasizes the importance of cyber laws in today's digital world and provides insights into how PECA contributes to maintaining law and order in cyberspace.
